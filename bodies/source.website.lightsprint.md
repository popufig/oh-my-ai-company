## What This Source Says

The homepage positions Lightsprint as one AI-native platform for product teams to plan, build, and ship together. It emphasizes real codebases rather than prototypes or sandboxes.

Key claims:

- Works with GitHub, Linear, Slack.
- Plan visually, run background cloud agents, preview and ship.
- Every change gets a live URL and opens a pull request.
- Lightsprint is model- and tool-agnostic.
- It claims to work on the real codebase, not a sandbox or prototype.
- It frames institutional memory as compounding: plans, requirements, task breakdowns, and decisions feed a growing memory of codebase patterns and preferences.
