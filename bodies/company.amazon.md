# Amazon

[[company.amazon]] 在 [[company.adept]] 研究中的关键关系是 2024-06-28 的 talent/license deal：Amazon hired Adept co-founders and some team members, and licensed Adept agent technology, multimodal models, and datasets under a non-exclusive license.

这不是公开证据意义上的完整收购。更准确说法：Amazon 吸收了 Adept 的核心 AGI/agent 人才和部分技术资产，Adept 继续独立运营。

Evidence: [[source.adept-update-amazon-2024-06-28]], [[source.reuters-amazon-adept-2024-06-28]], [[source.geekwire-amazon-adept-2024-06-28]].
