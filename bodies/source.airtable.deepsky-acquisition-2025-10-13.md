# Airtable DeepSky acquisition and David Azose CTO

Airtable announced David Azose as CTO, formerly leading ChatGPT Business Products at OpenAI, and the acquisition of DeepSky, an AI superagent for complex research and analysis.

DeepSky founding team: Chris Chang, Forrest Moret, Mark Huang, plus 12 employees joining Airtable. This is a core origin story for Airtable's Superagent/Hyperagent strategy.
