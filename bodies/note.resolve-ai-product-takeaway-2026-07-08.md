# Resolve AI product takeaway

Resolve AI fits the “agent substrate stack” as a production debugging / AI SRE layer. The product does not claim to be a generic chatbot over logs; both homepage and Lightspeed describe structured investigations across code, infrastructure, and telemetry.

What seems important:
- It mirrors how expert production engineers investigate incidents.
- It targets alert handling, RCA, and production debugging.
- It exposes MCP/API/Skills, so customers can plug in existing tools and tribal knowledge.
- Enterprise security/compliance are front-and-center, which makes sense because production debugging requires sensitive operational access.
