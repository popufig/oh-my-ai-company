# Lightspeed: AI Will Eat Services

URL: https://lsvp.com/stories/ai-will-eat-services/
Published: 2024-11-18
Byline: Dev Khare

Official Lightspeed/LSIP thesis article. Note the author emails are LSIP and the article emphasizes India; keep LSVP/LSIP distinction in mind.

Key points:
- Software TAM expands from productivity tools to the work itself, opening a >$10T TAM according to the article.
- It defines AI-led services as strong technology products with humans in the loop.
- Categories of AI work replacement include SaaS companies adding agents, full-stack AI agent companies, and AI services with humans in the loop.
- It emphasizes feedback loops, new demand from 10x cheaper/faster work, outcome quality, platform defensibility, focus, and workflow integration.
- Caution: services competition includes internal employees, outsourcing giants, agencies, freelancers, vertical SaaS and agent startups.

Use as a lens for Reindeer AI and other service-agent companies.
