# TechCrunch: Amazon hires founders away from Adept

Source: TechCrunch / Kyle Wiggers
Published: 2024-06-28
Evidence: S2 third-party / quality full

Key facts:

- Amazon licensed Adept technology and hired co-founders/team members.
- TechCrunch says David Luan joined Amazon along with Adept co-founders Augustus Odena, Maxwell Nye, Erich Elsen, Kelsey Szot and other employees.
- Adept was not closing; [[person.zach-brock]] took over as CEO as Adept refocused on agentic AI solutions.
- Adept had raised over $415M and reached around $1B valuation.
- TechCrunch explicitly notes Adept lost Ashish Vaswani and Niki Parmar early and struggled to bring product to market despite testing.
- Competitor examples mentioned by TechCrunch: Orby, Emergence and others.
