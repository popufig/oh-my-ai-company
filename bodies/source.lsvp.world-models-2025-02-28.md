# Lightspeed: Hello, World Models!

URL: https://lsvp.com/stories/hello-world-models/
Published: 2025-02-28
Byline: Faraz Fatemi

Official Lightspeed AI thesis article focused on world models, gaming and interactive media.

Key facts:
- At the time of publication, Lightspeed said it had invested about $2.5B in over 100 AI technology-stack companies. Later 2025 fundraise source updates this to 165 AI-native companies and over $5.5B invested into AI.
- Article maps Lightspeed AI exposure from foundation models to AI-native developer tooling and applications across enterprise, healthcare, fintech, consumer, gaming and interactive media.
- It highlights portfolio examples including Inworld, Pika and Suno.
- Core thesis: world models may become generative or engine-less video games and eventually support physical-world reasoning, robotics and interactive simulations.
- Near-term caution: state/memory limitations, playability, legal/copyright, 3D data needs and compute remain constraints.
