# Minds Builder Hub investment programme page

Source: https://build.hellominds.ai/program
Collected: 2026-07-09
Evidence: S1 official / quality full

Builder Hub states that the Minds Investment Programme backs early-stage teams where Minds is a core product layer, not a wrapper.

Value proposition for builders:

- Funding allocation is quality-driven and not fixed-schedule.
- Support includes LLM tokens/Cognition Credits, distribution, hands-on support, portfolio reach.
- Distribution promise: ship to an existing user base via the Minds Bazaar; skills are discoverable on day one.
- DevRel support: direct access to Minds product and DevRel team.
- Network: introductions across Animoca Brands' 600+ company portfolio.
- Funding may be a mix of cash and Cognition Credits, sized to the opportunity.
