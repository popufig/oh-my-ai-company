# Andrew Busse interview on Hyperagent

Andrew Busse, VP AI Operations at Airtable/Hyperagent, describes Hyperagent as an autonomous agent platform and argues that intelligence is the prerequisite, not the product. The product is the harness around the model: skills, memories/retrieval, evals/verification, model routing, team governance.

The interview also describes Founding 500: $10M inference credits to 500 founders, and says the campaign itself was run almost entirely agentically.
