# Adept announces $350M of new capital

Source: https://www.adept.ai/press/press-release-series-b/
Published: 2023-03-14
Evidence: S1 official / quality full

Adept announced $350M in Series B financing led by [[investor.general-catalyst]] and co-led by [[investor.spark-capital]], with participation from Addition, Greylock, Atlassian Ventures, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Workday Ventures, Caterina Fake, Frontiers Capital, PSP Growth, SV Angel, A.Capital and others.

Key facts:

- Series B amount: $350M.
- Previous Series A: $65M in early 2022.
- Use of proceeds: productization, model training/fine-tuning, headcount growth.
- Product thesis: large text-to-actions model that automates complex software workflows.
- ACT-1 framed as a foundation model able to interpret high-level natural language requests on software tools and perform tasks directly.
