# Forbes: Adept raises $350M to build AI that learns how to use software

Source: Forbes / Kenrick Cai
Published: 2023-03-14
Evidence: S2 third-party / quality partial because article has subscription wall after accessible excerpts

- Adept was roughly one year old and had about 25 employees at the time.

- Adept studied how humans use computers, from browsing the internet to navigating enterprise tools, to build an AI model that can turn text commands into actions.

- ACT-1 was described as an overlay window on top of existing software like Chrome or Salesforce.
