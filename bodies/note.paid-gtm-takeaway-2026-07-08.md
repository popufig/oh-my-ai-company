# Paid GTM takeaway

Paid's launch is a strong case of treating public传播 as an elevator pitch and distribution campaign.

What they did:
- Worked backward from a launch party date.
- Prepared PR, TechCrunch, founder posts, investor/customer attendance, website, brand, video, and messaging before launch day.
- Made the villain simple: seat-based SaaS pricing is broken for AI agents.
- Used Manny Medina's founder network and investor help to secure attention.
- Owned a launch week with multiple content beats rather than a one-day post.

Reported output from [[source.blog.paid-launch-playbook-2025]]:
- 24h: 15.8K visitors / 47K views.
- >650 signups, >140 scheduled meetings.
- >1000 signups within a week.
- Manny announcement: 532K impressions.

Takeaway for our product research method: when a company has a founder with strong prior reputation, we should not analyze launch channel performance as if it were generic content marketing. Founder credibility and investor/media access are part of the GTM asset base.
