## What This Source Says

YC describes Lightsprint as collaborative product development with cloud agents. It is a platform for software teams to plan visually, preview live, and ship with cloud agents. The page explicitly says non-engineers can now ship end to end reliably.

Founder/team facts captured:

- Founded in 2026.
- Batch: YC Spring 2026 / P26.
- Team size: 3.
- Location: San Francisco.
- Founders: Ben Ong, Benedict Chan, Heng Hong Lee.
- Ben Ong: AI investor/product background at SEA Group and Temasek.
- Benedict Chan: VP Engineering at Chainlink and CTO at BitGo.
- Heng Hong Lee: built Facebook Messenger in 2013 and was Director of Engineering at Fazz.

The founder Q&A frames the core problem as product changes waiting behind engineering sprints while coding agents only speed up engineers, not the whole product team.
