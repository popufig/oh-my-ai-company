# Tasklet email and iMessage

URL: https://tasklet.ai/release-notes/2026-01-21-email-and-imessage
Legacy local source: `vertical/media/knowledge/radar/tasklet/sources/release-notes.cn.md`.

Evidence level: S1 official release note. Quality: full via legacy capture.

Key points:
- Tasklet agents can contact teammates, clients and vendors via email or iMessage.
- Two-way: replies go back to the agent, which can process and act on them.
- Intended use cases: reports, delegation, collaboration, notifications, daily briefs.
- Important for "agent reaches humans" and approval/notification loops.
