# Reuters: Amazon lures cofounders from Adept

Source: Reuters
Published: 2024-06-28
Evidence: S2 third-party / quality full

Key facts:

- Amazon hired Adept co-founders and some team members.
- Adept said cofounder/CEO [[person.david-luan]] and several co-founders/employees were leaving to join Amazon.
- Adept had raised over $410M and was valued above $1B.
- Amazon paid a licensing fee to use some Adept technology; deal terms were not disclosed.
- About 20 Adept workers remained at the startup according to Amazon spokesperson.
- Luan and others report to Rohit Prasad in Amazon AGI; some join devices/services.
- Reuters notes similar structure to Microsoft/Inflection talent-license deal and regulatory scrutiny context.
