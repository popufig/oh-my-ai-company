# Lightspeed: The AI Agent Economy Has a $19 Trillion Problem

URL: https://lsvp.com/stories/the-ai-agent-economy-has-a-19-trillion-problem-our-investment-in-paid/
Published: 2025-09-29
Byline: Alexander Schmitt

Official investment announcement/thesis for Paid.

Key facts:
- Lightspeed led Paid's $21M seed round.
- Lightspeed argues AI agent builders undercharge because they cannot prove financial impact and track dynamic agent work economics.
- Paid is framed as the missing business system for AI agent economics.
- Capabilities listed: customer value proof, custom pricing, outcome/hybrid models, cost tracking, AI business intelligence.
- Paid customers/examples mentioned include Artisan, Boon and IFS.
- Lightspeed sees the next wave of AI value in infrastructure that operationalizes AI deployment and application companies that capture value created.
