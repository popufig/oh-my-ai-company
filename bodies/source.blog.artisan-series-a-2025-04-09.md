# Artisan raises $25M Series A

Artisan 官方博客，作者 Jaspar Carmichael-Jack，发布时间 2025-04-09。

关键信息：
- 宣布 $25M Series A，Glade Brook Capital lead；HubSpot Ventures、Oliver Jung、Day One Ventures、BOND、Soma Capital、Sequoia Scout 等参与；Y Combinator 继续支持。
- 使命表达：replace repetitive work with AI employees。
- Ava：discovers leads、researches them、writes and sends hyper-personalized messages across email、books meetings automatically。
- 技术表达：multi-agent system + real-time context engine，抓取 job changes、new funding、leadership moves 等 buying signals。
- SumUp case：官方称 8-15 positive replies per week from hard-to-reach SMBs。
- Autonomy roadmap：Level 1 human supervision；Level 2 independent outreach；building toward Level 3；long-term Level 5 Artisans。
- 新产品计划：Aaron inbound SDR、Aria meeting assistant，目标成为 end-to-end AI sales system。
- Team：Ming Li joined as CTO；previous VP Technology at Deel and engineering leadership at Rippling、TikTok、Google；also brought four senior engineers from Rippling。

研究价值：Series A 文是 Artisan 从单一 Ava 走向 AI sales system / AI employee workforce 的核心叙事文档。
