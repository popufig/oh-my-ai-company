# Lightspeed: Billing infrastructure in the age of AI agents

URL: https://lsvp.com/stories/billing-infrastructure-in-the-age-of-co-pilots-and-ai-agents/
Published: 2025-03-06
Byline: Lightspeed Venture Partners

Official Lightspeed thesis article.

Key points:
- As software moves from human per-seat usage to human-to-machine and machine-to-machine interactions, pricing needs to evolve.
- AI agents make traditional SaaS pricing weaker because value and cost are tied to dynamic actions, compute, API calls, transactions and outcomes.
- Usage/performance pricing needs robust data infrastructure: metering, rating, invoicing and finance integration in near real time.
- Lightspeed frames billing infrastructure as a strategic battleground and predicts a data observability layer for monetization.
- CRM, CPQ, CLM, procurement, billing/invoicing, revenue management and ERP all need to adapt around usage-based AI economics.

Use this as the generic thesis behind Lightspeed's investment in Paid and other AI monetization infrastructure.
