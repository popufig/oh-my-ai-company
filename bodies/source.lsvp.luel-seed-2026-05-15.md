# Lightspeed: investment in Luel multimodal AI training data marketplace

URL: https://lsvp.com/stories/our-investment-in-luel-the-marketplace-for-multimodal-ai-training-data/
Published: 2026-05-15
Byline: Lisa Han and Raviraj Jain

Official Lightspeed investment announcement.

Key facts:
- Lightspeed led Luel's $31.2M financing.
- Luel is described as a neutral two-sided marketplace for rights-cleared multimodal AI training data.
- Customers include generative AI labs, robotics companies and speech research teams.
- Luel claims a contributor network of 500K+ people across 96 countries.
- Thesis: frontier AI's bottleneck shifts from scraping public web to collecting licensed, high-quality, multimodal, human-generated data with provenance and QA.

This is a new portfolio seed candidate for our map because it sits at the data supply layer for frontier AI, voice agents, robotics and video/world models.
