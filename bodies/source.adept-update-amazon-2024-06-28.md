# An update from Adept

Source: https://www.adept.ai/blog/adept-update/
Published: 2024-06-28
Evidence: S1 official / quality full

Adept announced a major strategic change:

- Adept had been building larger multimodal foundation models, fine-tuning them into agents, and building enterprise agent products.
- Adept said it had deployed product in mission-critical production environments and automated end-to-end workflows with dozens of steps.
- Continuing both foundation model work and enterprise agent product work would require too much fundraising attention for foundation models.
- Adept would focus entirely on solutions that enable agentic AI, powered by in-house models, agentic data, web interaction software, and custom infrastructure.
- Adept co-founders and some team members joined [[company.amazon]] AGI organization.
- Amazon licensed Adept’s agent technology, state-of-the-art multimodal models, and a few datasets.
- [[person.zach-brock]] became CEO; [[person.tim-weingarten]] remained Head of Product.

Research value: this is the official pivot point. Adept is not simply “acquired”; it remains independent but after a major talent/technology transfer to Amazon.
