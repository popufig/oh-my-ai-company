# Reuters: Harvey valued at 11B

S2 Reuters article, published 2026-03-25. Confirms $200M at $11B valuation, total funding more than $1B, co-led by GIC and Sequoia with Andreessen Horowitz and Coatue participating.

Reuters notes Harvey develops legal AI tools for law firms and corporate legal departments, helping automate complex workflows such as contract analysis, due diligence, compliance, and litigation. It also mentions Harvey raised $160M at $8B valuation in December 2025 Series F led by Andreessen Horowitz.
