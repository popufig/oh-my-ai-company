# Animoca Brands and Ethoswarm launch Animoca Minds

Source: https://www.animocabrands.com/announcement/animoca-brands-and-ethoswarm-launch-animoca-minds-to-accelerate-agentic-economy
Published: 2026-02-05
Evidence: S1 official / quality full

Official launch announcement. [[company.animoca-brands]] and CryptoSlam's [[company.ethoswarm]] announced a strategic partnership to launch Animoca Minds, a service enabling users to deploy and operate persistent AI agents.

Key facts:

- Animoca Minds was launched on 2026-02-05.
- Powered by the Ethoswarm protocol.
- Target: non-technical users who do not want to run local servers or manage hardware.
- Early access was originally at animocaminds.com.
- Users “awaken” a personalized always-on AI agent in the cloud.
- Specialized agent profiles mentioned: market researcher, crypto scout.
- Blockchain is framed as identity, economics, and provenance layer; complex blockchain interfaces are abstracted away.
- Interaction channels: email and Telegram, with more messaging apps planned.

People explicitly quoted:

- [[person.randy-wasinger]], founder and CEO of CryptoSlam, on Ethoswarm making persistent/safe AI agents available to everyday users.
- [[person.mohamed-ezeldin]], Head of Animoca Labs, on democratizing persistent sovereign agents beyond engineers/private servers.
- [[person.yat-siu]], Animoca co-founder/executive chairman, on agents adapting to users and blockchain enabling agency/ownership/settlement.
