# Animoca launches up to US$10M Minds investment programme

Source: https://www.animocabrands.com/announcement/animoca-brands-launches-up-to-us-10m-investment-programme-for-developers-building-with-persistent-ai-agent-platform-minds
Published: 2026-05-05
Evidence: S1 official / quality full

Official announcement that [[company.animoca-brands]] will invest up to US$10M in selected projects building with [[company.hellominds]], also known as Animoca Minds.

Important distinction: this is not a disclosed financing round for HelloMinds itself. It is an ecosystem investment programme for teams building products where Minds is a core product layer.

Programme facts:

- Up to US$10M allocation.
- Applications open at https://build.hellominds.ai/program.
- Targets early-stage teams with clear product thesis, execution capability, and practical path to deployable/scalable products.
- Vertical range: gaming, finance, productivity, social, and other applications, as long as they leverage Minds as a core product layer.
- Quality-driven, no fixed schedule.
- Support beyond capital: platform support, Cognition Credits, access to Minds technical team.
- Distribution/network: access to Animoca’s 600+ Web3 company/project portfolio for partnerships and distribution.
- Product capabilities emphasized: persistent context, collaborative intelligence, frictionless access, agentic sovereignty.
