# Harvey growth round at 11B valuation

S1 official blog, published 2026-03-25. Harvey announced $200M at an $11B valuation. Round co-led by GIC and Sequoia, with participation from Andreessen Horowitz, Coatue, Conviction Partners, Elad Gil, Evantic, and Kleiner Perkins.

Use of funds: expand more than 25,000 custom agents customers run on Harvey and scale embedded legal engineering teams inside top law firms and legal departments.
