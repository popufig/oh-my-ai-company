# Forbes: Startup raises $6.5M for AI employee payments

Forbes article by Elaine Pofeldt. Clean browser read succeeded.

Key facts:
- Describes AIsa as a San Francisco-based payment startup.
- Reports $6.5M funding, with new seed led by Alibaba and Tribe Capital; Draper Associates, Sumitomo Corporation and Saison Capital participated.
- Describes product as a unified transaction layer / API key for agents and developers to discover, access and pay for digital resources with usage-based billing and fiat/stablecoin settlement.
- Quotes Jordan Liu’s “Amazon for agents” framing.
- Says the team has 10 people.
- Says target users include one-person companies and small teams.
- Reports Jordan previously founded a PayPal-like digital wallet for unbanked customers in Southeast Asia and a blockchain wallet.
- Reports more than 20,000 registered agents without paid marketing. This conflicts with the official announcement’s 50,000+ registered agents and must be kept as separate source wording.
