# BusinessWire: Lightspeed closes over $9B in new funds

URL: https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251215319417/en/Lightspeed-Closes-Over-%249B-in-New-Funds-Aimed-at-Backing-Category-Definers-Worldwide
Published: 2025-12-15

BusinessWire release. Treat as S2 distribution of Lightspeed announcement.

Key facts:
- Lightspeed closed more than $9B across six vehicles, including Fund XV-A $980M, Fund XV-B $1.2B, Select VI $1.8B, Opportunity Fund III $3.3B, Co-Investment Fund I $600M, and $1.25B single-investor vehicles in 2025.
- Says Lightspeed manages over $40B in assets.
- Says it first invested in AI in 2012.
- Claims 165 AI-native companies backed and more than $5.5B invested into AI, with most capital committed at Seed, Series A and Series B.
- Names AI-related companies including Abridge, Anthropic, Anduril, Castelion, Databricks, Glean, Mistral, Thinking Machines, Reflection AI, Skild AI and others.
- Ravi Mhatre quote frames AI as transformative and cites shifts in professional services, scientific discovery and autonomy.
