# Reindeer AI product takeaway

Reindeer fits Lightspeed's “AI will eat services” thesis: AI-led services or agentic operations systems can go after work itself, not just productivity software.

The interesting product details are:
- 200+ modular pre-trained AI skills.
- Human-in-the-loop thresholds and reasoning traces.
- Inner loop: expert feedback updates logic and reruns cases for SME verification.
- Outer loop: recurring exception patterns become proposed change bundles.
- Enterprise deployment: runs in customer infrastructure and integrates with SAP/Oracle/NetSuite/Workday/ServiceNow.

This makes Reindeer closer to “systems of work” than point automation. It may suit high-volume operational processes where customers need auditability, controlled escalation, and changing business logic.
