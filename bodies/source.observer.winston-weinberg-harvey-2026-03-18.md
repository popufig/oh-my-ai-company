# Observer: Winston Weinberg on Harvey and Big Law AI

S2 interview/profile, published 2026-03-18. It says Winston Weinberg was a junior associate at O'Melveny & Myers and Gabe Pereyra was a research scientist at Meta when they began experimenting with early GPT-3.

Early GTM: finding futurists at A&O Shearman, Deutsche Telekom, PwC; heavy focus on security and governance. It says Harvey integrates LexisNexis content/citations, works with law schools, and plans office expansion in Singapore, Dallas, Dublin, Paris, Bangalore, in addition to Sydney, San Francisco, New York, London, Toronto.

Differentiation against model companies: security, governance, collaboration, memory, integrations.
