# TechFundingNews: Duvo $15M seed

发布日期：2025-12-02。

TechFundingNews 报道 Duvo raised $15M seed led by [[investor.index-ventures]]，with [[investor.credo-ventures]]、[[investor.northzone]]、[[investor.puzzle-ventures]] and angels。

报道称 Duvo 面向 retail operations，integrates with SAP、email、portal、spreadsheet tools，without coding or large IT projects；AI agents handle end-to-end workflows, reducing manual work by up to 40%。

报道补充了 founder quote：Duvo tends to compete with systems retailers already have: RPA platforms, SAP automation tools, generic workflow builders, or consulting projects trying to stitch legacy systems together。
